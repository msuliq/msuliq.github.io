About me

A software developer, former commodity trader, and linguist.

I'm a programming enthusiast and self-taught web developer who likes learning new languages and technologies that allow solving complex problems and building robust applications. I started learning Ruby in January 2022 and in six months got offered an internship. Currently I am working as Junior Software Developer at Midstay. I learn Ruby, Python, SQL, JS, HTML and CSS through online courses, books and video tutorials. I like solving puzzles and figuring out non-standard solutions. In the long run, I would like to expand by skillset by adding C, C++ and Rust to the list of languages I work with and I would love to try myself in fintech, ML and AI.

In past I have worn many hats, I was a foreign exchange student at a US high school, majored in linguistics at college, worked as interpreter, served in administrative and managerial roles at an oil company, tried myself in commodity trading and then switched career path to software engineering.
